Rails Apps

  • Trello Clone

    A project management app, based on Trello. Made with Rails 5 and Backbone.js.

    Subscribed users can manage their accounts, update payment info, upload images, and create boards, lists, and cards. Cards can hold a variety of information, such as notes, due dates, labels, images, and activity. Cards can be moved between lists, and lists can be moved between boards via drag & drop functionality.

  • NASA Live Feed

    A live streaming video and chatbot app. Made with Rails 5 and ReactJS.

    Users can login to their Google account with OAuth, watch live streaming videos, and add messages to YouTube chat streams that update in realtime.

  • Yelp Clone

    A review app, based on Yelp. Made with Rails 5.

    Users can register for accounts, edit account info, make credit card payments, setup business pages, search businesses, review businesses, upload photos, send messages to other users, befriend other users, follow other users, and interact on forums.

  • Synth Forum

    A single-page forum app for synthesizer enthusiasts, based on Reddit. Made with Rails 5 and Ember.js.

    Users can manage accounts, post topics in forums, comment on topics, and send messages to other users.

  • Personal Planner

    A personal planner app, featuring an address book, calendar, note pad, and todo list. Made with Rails 4.

    Users can register for accounts; edit account info; and create and edit contacts, notes, events and todos. Notes, events and todos appear on the calendar on the appropriate date.
